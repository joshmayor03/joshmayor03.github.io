My Projects

n8n · Content · 6 workflows

"Straight Facts" FB Automation

How it works: a six-workflow n8n pipeline that runs a Facebook content page end to end. An AI step drafts the caption and generates the matching image (OpenRouter, Gemini 2.5 Flash Image), the post then moves through an approval workflow and a grading step to filter out weak drafts, and an approved post is queued and published automatically on schedule. All post data lives in Supabase — it was originally tracked in Google Sheets, migrated over as the volume grew.

n8n · HR Ops

Holiday Email Automation

How it works: the workflow checks a table of upcoming Philippine public holidays and, when one's approaching, auto-generates a formal HR memo in the company's official template. That draft routes to HR by email with one-click Approve/Reject buttons; once approved, it's distributed automatically to hundreds of employees across the company's domains — no more manual copy-paste memos or delayed holiday notices.
